Our Mission

To represent God on earth, helping others to discover and drink from the fountain of abundant life. This mission is supported by biblical references such as 2 Corinthians 5:20, 1 John 4:17, and Matthew 28:1.

Our Vision

To raise healthy Christians, Spirit, Soul and body, equipped to give direction to creation. This vision emphasizes the holistic development of individuals in their spiritual, emotional, and physical aspects.
